NO HUMAN IS LIMITED

Eliud Kipchoge proved No Human Is Limited when he became the first human in history to run a sub-two-hour marathon in the city of Vienna, Austria on the morning of 12th October 2019. Kipchoge ran the 42.195 KM distance in a remarkable time of 1:59:40. This blog is in two parts, in the first part I looked at the two fastest unofficial marathon times ran by Eliud in Breaking 2 and during the Ineos Challenge.
