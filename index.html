TIMOTHY MADEGWA

My name is Timothy Madegwa, I pursued a Bachelor of Science in Mathematics and Computer Science at Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology (J.K.U.A.T) Karen Campus and graduated with a Second Class (Upper Division) in November 2017. I am self taught Machine Learning Engineer (using Python) and I am passionate about all things Machine Learning.

I am interested in working with companies with customer data to improve on their customer experiences by harnessing the power of Data Analytics and Machine Learning. From my background in Mathematics and Computer Science, I gained knowledge in computer programming, statistical analysis, mathematical modelling, data visualizations, enabling me to analyze and get insights from data.
I participated in the Zindi transport challenge with the aim of predicting the number of tickets sold by a travel company to various parts of rural Kenya and was ranked among the top 150 competitors.

I am a hardworking, passionate and self-driven young man ready to take on the challenges of the work force so as to make my country better.
One of my major goals in life is to create Machine Learning software that will be a that will impact the Health Care sector especially in the early diagnosis of diseases and predictive tests that will avoid long, painful tests.
