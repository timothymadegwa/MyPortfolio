KOPAPAP

KopaPap an application that does instant credit scoring for an individual/ institution (SME) in Kenya (based on Machine learning) and give lower interest rates on loans and overdrafts as compared to digital lenders. It is built using Python, Flask, Sci-kit learn and Finastra’s Onboarding and Information API. The algorithm predicts the probability that the applicant will pay back a loan based on a pre-trained model. Click here to demo KopaPap.
